About Krishna Group

Established in 1989, KRISHNA is a group started under the name of SHRIKRISHNA CAREER ACADEMY and within a short span it has become the group of experts from various fields like non-academic education, career counseling, human resource, quality & manufacturing systems, marketing and likewise. Today KRISHNA is involved in language skill building, a full-fledged career counseling center, ISO certifications, KAIZEN process establishment and software training. Training & Placement is a venture, which is designed to suit Industry needs where HR & Technical experts actually participate to shape participants.


Our Quality Policy
